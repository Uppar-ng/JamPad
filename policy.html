1. Introduction

Primer Technologies ("Company," "we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile application, and services (collectively, the "Platform").

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency and trust. We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is yours, and we're just temporary custodians helping you find or list properties.

By using the Platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Property Listings

  • Property address
  • Property photos
  • Rental price
  • Property details
  • Amenities list

Payment Information

  • Payment card details (processed securely)
  • Billing address
  • Transaction history

Communications

  • Messages to landlords/tenants
  • Customer support inquiries
  • Feedback and reviews

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information

Usage Information

  • Pages viewed
  • Search queries
  • Properties viewed/saved
  • Time spent on Platform

Location Information

  • Approximate location (from IP)
  • Exact location (with permission)
  • Search locations

Cookie Data

  • Browser cookies

  • Session data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Enable property search, listings, and communication between landlords and tenants
  • To Improve Our Platform: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To Communicate: Send updates, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support
  • To Process Payments: Handle subscriptions and premium features securely
  • To Verify Listings: Confirm property authenticity and landlord identity
  • To Personalize Experience: Show relevant property recommendations
  • To Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • To Comply with Law: Meet legal obligations and regulatory requirements

We only use your information for purposes that are necessary, proportionate, and consistent with this policy.

4. Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share information in these limited circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users

  • Landlords: Your name and phone number are visible to tenants who view your listings
  • Tenants: Your name and inquiry details are shared with landlords you contact
  • Reviews: Your reviews are public but can be anonymous

4.2 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third parties who help us operate:

  • Payment processors: To handle transactions securely
  • Cloud hosting: To store data
  • Analytics: To understand platform usage
  • Customer support: To manage support tickets

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety of users.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Here's what we use:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Preference Cookies Remember your settings (theme, filters, favorites) 1 year
Analytics Cookies Track usage to improve our platform 2 years
Marketing Cookies Personalize ads (opt-out available) 90 days

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

By continuing to use our Platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS (HTTPS)
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed and salted, never stored in plain text
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability testing
  • Data Minimization: We only collect what's necessary

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to improve our protections.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of your data

Rectification

Correct inaccurate information

Deletion

Request deletion of your data

Limit how we use your data

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  1. Go to Account Settings (for direct access to some data)
  2. Email us at get.primer@proton.me
  3. Contact our Data Protection Officer

We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days. Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide you with services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Account Data: Retained until you delete your account. After deletion, we may retain limited information as required by law.

Listing Data: Retained while your listing is active and for a reasonable period after deactivation for record-keeping.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a minor without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Nigeria, where our servers and service providers are located. These countries may have different data protection laws.

When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses and data processing agreements.

By using our Platform, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications (for significant changes)
  • Displaying a notice on the Platform

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
