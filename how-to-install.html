How to Install Our App

Follow these simple steps to install Primer Technologies on your device

1

Open in Chrome

Using Google Chrome on your Android device, go to the Primer website. Select "Install app for Android" to begin the installation.

2

Click Install Prompt

On the redirected page, tap the "Install" button in the banner that displays the app icon.

3

Open Browser Menu

Tap the three-dot menu button in Chrome and select "Add to Home screen".

4

Select the app

Go ahead and select the app icon to start the installation. It might take about a minute to complete—just keep an eye on your notifications for the confirmation.

5

Go to your apps

You're all set! Simply search for 'Primer' or find it in your app list, then tap to open it—just like any other app.

1

Open in Safari

Open the Primer website in Safari browser on your iPhone and tap install for iPhone.

2

Tap Share Button

Tap the share button (square with arrow) at the bottom of the screen.

3

Add to Home Screen

Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" from the share menu options.

4

Confirm Name

Edit the app name if you'd like, then tap "Add" in the top right.

5

App Added!

The app icon appears on your home screen. Tap it to launch Primer anytime!
