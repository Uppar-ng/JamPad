ABOUT US

Making Housing Smarter in Nigeria

We're on a mission to transform the Nigerian housing market through transparency, verification, and technology.

Our Mission

"To create a trusted platform that connects verified tenants with verified properties, eliminating scams and making the rental process transparent, efficient, and accessible for everyone in Nigeria."

Our Story

Primer Technologies was born from a simple observation: finding quality housing in Nigeria, especially for students and young professionals, was unnecessarily difficult. Scams, fake listings, unreliable landlords, and lack of transparency made the process stressful and time-consuming.

In November 2025, our founder experienced this firsthand while helping a younger sibling find accommodation near Kaduna State University. After weeks of frustration, wasted time, and near-scams, the idea for Primer was born.

Since launching on November 14, 2025, we've grown rapidly, connecting multiple tenants with verified properties across Kaduna State. Today, just three months later, we're proud to be Nigeria's fastest-growing trusted housing platform.

"I knew there had to be a better way. Nigerians deserve a housing platform they can trust. That's why we built Primer."

— Alamin Ahmed, Founder
