Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes! Primer is completely free to use. You only pay providers directly for their services.

How do I install the app?

Visit our website on your mobile browser, tap share, and select "Add to Home Screen". That's it!

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed! Just browse and contact providers directly via call or WhatsApp.

How are providers verified?

We verify all providers through background checks, ID verification, and in-person interviews.

Can I become a provider?

Yes! Contact us through our app or website to learn about joining our platform.

What areas do you cover?

We currently serve Kaduna, Lagos, Abuja, and expanding to more cities across Nigeria.
