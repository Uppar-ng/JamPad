QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the Primer process.

How long does verification take?

Property verification typically takes 24-48 hours. Our team visits each property physically to confirm details.

Is it really free for tenants?

Yes! Primer is completely free for tenants. You can search, view, and contact landlords at no cost.

Can I list multiple properties?

Absolutely. Landlords can list multiple properties and manage them all from a single dashboard.

What does "24/7 Electricity" mean?

Properties with this badge have guaranteed power supply, either from grid or backup generators.
