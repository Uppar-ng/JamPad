1. Introduction

Welcome to Primer Technologies ("Primer," "we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Progressive Web App (PWA) and website.

🇳🇬 Nigerian Compliance: This policy complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and other applicable Nigerian privacy laws.

2. Intellectual Property & Provider Information

Provider Contact Information is Primer's Intellectual Property PROTECTED

All contact information of service providers listed on Primer, including but not limited to:

  • Phone numbers and WhatsApp contacts
  • Email addresses
  • Business addresses and locations
  • Social media handles
  • Business names and descriptions
  • Photos and portfolio images
  • Service rates and pricing information

are the exclusive intellectual property of Primer Technologies and are protected under Nigerian Copyright Law.

2.1 Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Web scraping, data mining, or automated extraction of any provider information
  • Copying, reproducing, or republishing provider contact details on any other platform
  • Creating competing platforms using information obtained from Primer
  • Selling or commercializing provider information to third parties

3. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our app, including:

3.1 Personal Data

  • Name and contact data: Your full name, email address, phone number, and location (city)
  • Credentials: Account information if you choose to create a profile
  • Communications: Records of your interactions with providers
  • Payment information: We do NOT store payment details

3.2 Usage Data

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage patterns: Services viewed, providers contacted
  • Location data: General location (city-level)

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Connect you with service providers
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Communicate with you about services
  • Protect against unauthorized access and scraping
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only with:

  • Service Providers: When you contact them (name, phone, location)

6. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data via HTTPS/SSL
  • Access controls: Strict internal access to user data
  • Anti-scraping measures: Advanced detection systems

7. Your Data Protection Rights

  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing: Object to data use
