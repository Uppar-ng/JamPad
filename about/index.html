About Primer

Our Story

Primer was born from a simple observation: finding reliable service providers in Nigeria was too difficult. People needed movers, cleaners, handymen, and other professionals but didn't know where to look or who to trust.

We created Primer to solve this problem. Our platform connects you directly with vetted service providers in your area. No complicated booking systems, no middlemen. Just browse, call, and get your work done.

Today, Primer helps thousands of Nigerians find trusted professionals for moving, cleaning, repairs, and more.
