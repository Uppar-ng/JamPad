How do I list my property?
Simply contact us via phone, email, or the form above. Our team will guide you through the process and help upload your property details.
Is there a cost to list?
Contact us for current pricing information. We offer flexible options for landlords of all sizes.

Our team will schedule a visit to your property, verify ownership documentation, and confirm all listed amenities. This builds trust with potential tenants.

Once your property is listed, tenants can contact you directly via phone through the app. You'll receive notifications for new inquiries.
