Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about finding homes, listing properties, and using Primer.

General Questions

What is Primer?

Primer is Nigeria's trusted housing platform that connects verified tenants with verified properties. We list apartments, houses, and student accommodations across Nigeria, with a focus on transparency and reliability.

Every property on Primer is physically verified by our team to ensure no scams or fake listings.

Is Primer free to use?

For Tenants: Yes! Primer is completely free for tenants. You can search, view, and contact landlords at no cost.

For Landlords: Contact us for current pricing information. We offer flexible options for landlords of all sizes.

Which cities do you cover?

Currently, we have properties in:

  • Kaduna Our first and most powerful city.

We're expanding to more cities back to back! Contact us if you'd like to list properties in your area.

How do I install the app?

You can install the Primer app for/from:

  • iOS
  • Android

Click the Download App button in the navigation bar or visit this link to download.

For Tenants

How do I search for properties?

You can search properties by:

  • Location: Enter city, neighborhood, or university name
  • Price range: Set minimum and maximum budget
  • Property type: Apartment, house, studio, hostel, shared
  • Amenities: Filter for 24/7 electricity, security, WiFi, etc.
  • Bedrooms: 1, 2, 3+ bedrooms

Use the search bar on the home page or visit Explore to browse all properties.

How do I contact a landlord?

Each property listing has a direct contact button. You can:

  • Call the landlord directly through the app
  • Send an inquiry message
  • Schedule a viewing

Note: You don't need to create an account to contact landlords, but creating an account lets you save favorites.

What does "24/7 Electricity" mean?

Properties with the "24/7 Electricity" badge have guaranteed power supply. This means:

  • Connected to the national grid with reliable service
  • Backup generator for outages
  • Inverter or solar power systems in some cases

These properties have been verified by our team to ensure continuous power.

Are the properties really verified?

Yes! Every property on Primer is physically verified by our team before listing. We check:

  • Property actually exists at given address
  • Photos are accurate and recent
  • Amenities listed are available
  • Landlord is legitimate owner/agent

If you find an unverified or suspicious listing, please report it immediately via our contact form.

Can I save properties to view later?

Absolutely! Click the heart icon on any property to save it to your favorites. You can:

  • Access saved properties from the Favorites tab
  • Compare multiple properties side by side
  • Share saved properties with friends/family

Create a free account to sync your favorites across devices.

How do I find student housing?

Use the university filter to find properties near specific campuses:

  • Select your university from the dropdown
  • See properties ranked by distance from campus
  • Filter specifically for student hostels or shared accommodations

We partner with universities to ensure safe, verified student housing options.

For Landlords

How do I list my property?

To list your property:

  1. Contact us via our contact form
  2. Call us at +234 800 PRIMER
  3. Email us at get.primer@proton.me

Our team will guide you through the process and help upload your property details.

Is it really free to list?

Contact us for current pricing information. We offer flexible options for landlords of all sizes.

Our team will discuss the best plan for your needs during your consultation.

How does verification work?

Our verification process includes:

  1. Physical visit to the property address
  2. Verification of ownership documents
  3. Checking all listed amenities
  4. Taking verification photos
  5. Identity verification of landlord

Verified properties get a special badge and appear higher in search results.

How do I receive inquiries?

When tenants are interested in your property:

  • They can call you directly (your number is displayed)
  • You receive notifications in the app
  • View all inquiries in your landlord dashboard
  • Track how many views each property gets
Can I list multiple properties?

Yes! You can list any number of properties. Contact us to discuss bulk listing options and get special rates for multiple properties.

All properties can be managed through your landlord dashboard.

What is the landlord dashboard?

The landlord dashboard gives you:

  • Real-time view counts for each property
  • Inquiry tracking and management
  • Performance analytics
  • Bulk property editing

Access it from the app or web version after your properties are listed.

Verification

How do you verify properties?

Our verification process is thorough:

  1. Physical visit: Team member visits the property address
  2. Documentation: Verify ownership papers or agency agreement
  3. Photo verification: Take current photos to match listing
  4. Amenities check: Confirm all listed amenities exist
  5. Landlord ID: Verify landlord identity

Verification typically takes 24-48 hours.

What does the verified badge mean?

The verified badge means:

  • Property has been physically visited by our team
  • All information has been confirmed accurate
  • Landlord has been verified
  • Property meets our quality standards

Look for the blue verified badge when searching for properties!

How do I report a suspicious listing?

If you encounter a suspicious listing:

  1. Use our contact form to report it
  2. Call our support line immediately: +234 800 PRIMER
  3. Email us at get.primer@proton.me

We investigate all reports within 24 hours and take immediate action.

Account & Security

Do I need an account to use Primer?

No, you can browse and contact landlords without an account. However, creating a free account gives you:

  • Save favorites across devices
  • Get notifications for new listings
  • Track your inquiries
  • Faster contact with landlords
How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for reset instructions
  4. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive an email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes! We take security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted
  • We never share your information with third parties
  • Your phone number is only shown to verified users
  • We comply with Nigerian data protection laws

Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account anytime:

  1. Go to Profile Settings
  2. Click "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your decision

All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. Note that this action cannot be undone.
